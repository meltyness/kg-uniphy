#KeyConcept

### Description
Relative to a selected [[coordinate system]] a [[particle]]'s position is a [[vector quantity]].

Closely related to [[displacement]].

### [[Representation]]
#### Symbolic
$x_i, x(t)$

#### Graphical
$x\text{-}t$ graphs showing position over time.

### Related [[physical quantities]]
- [[velocity]]
- [[time]]
- [[displacement]]
