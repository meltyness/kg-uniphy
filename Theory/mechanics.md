#KeyConcept 

### Definition
The study of the relationships among [[force]], [[material]], and [[motion]].

This is further broken down into 

- [[kinematics]] describing [[motion]]
- [[dynamics]] which relates [[motion]] to [[what causes motion, or causality]]
